A Word From the Editor

Today, I celebrate the birth of a truly remarkable woman - my sister, Toluwa. It's been a year and almost a month since I knew her. And every moment with her has been more than I can really describe.

Toluwa is not just a sister, she's a mentor, a teacher, a confidante, a source of great and unwavering support. Toluwa pushes me more than i can do for myself, I really have to admit.

Career wise, she's there. She'll say, Tolu have you done this... You should apply for this. How far, how's it going? What are you using to learn? Even with all the rubbish I say sometimes, she'll still encourage me and say well done.

Personal growth? She's up for me. She'll say, send me your goals for the week. This is what I do. You should do it also. So many things I've learnt from her. She'll ask, which book are you currently reading? She'll say, are you tracking your goals daily. During our early days, she'll say, this podcast, you should listen to it too.

Academically, she'll ask, how many hours are you reading per day? How's it going? During exam times, she'll be on my neck.

Times when she'll say, are you fine... I know you're not fine, and she'll say, don't lie to me...

Food wise, she's a food adviser herself... She'll tell me eat apples, eat fruits. She'll say send me your meal timetable for the week.

Spiritual wise, she'll answer questions I ask. She'll say, Tolu, you should fast on this specific day this week. She'll say how many sermons are you listening to this week. You should stream midweek service oo.

Have you met someone who will not blame you for even your wrongs? Things you should beat yourself up for, she'll still pacify you. And tell you, next time, don't do that again.

Words cannot describe how much I'm grateful to God I have you. Many times I even wonder if I deserve someone like you. Like, if my efforts can match up to how much you want for me.

As you celebrate your birthday, I really want you to know how much you mean to me. I'm so proud to be your menteeee! 🥹

Your presence in my life, none like no other. Many times she should get angry at me. But she'll still not get angry. I'm so lucky to call you mine.

Thank youu for all you do, sister. God bless you so much. Happy Birthdayyyyyyy! Let's get the party started! 🌹

— Toluwanimi
